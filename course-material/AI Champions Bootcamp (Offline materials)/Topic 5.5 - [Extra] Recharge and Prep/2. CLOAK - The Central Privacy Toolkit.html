For instance, it includes a mock data generation feature that can create realistic data, perfect for building your Proof-of-Concept prototypes during the Project Phase.

What is Cloak?

  • Cloak is a Central Privacy Toolkit for public sector users. It is a one-stop, self-help portal that helps non-expert users transform datasets in accordance with public sector guidelines for data-sharing with non-government entities, and other custom use cases.

  • Cloak provides the following privacy tools:

    • Tabular anonymisation allows users to apply anonymisation techniques to, and address re-identification risk in compliance with policy guidelines.

    • Free-Text Anonymisation detects and redacts Personal Identifiable Information (PII) from unstructured data.

    • Mock Data Generation generates realistic data based on public datasets.

    • In future, Cloak will support other privacy-preserving technologies such as differential privacy and synthetic data generation.
